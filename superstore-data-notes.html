Data Notes

Additional context about the dataset, methodology, and assumptions used in the Superstore Sales Insights Dashboard project.

Project Context

This project was completed as part of an analytics exercise focused on exploring retail sales performance. The objective was to analyze sales and profit patterns across regions and product categories using interactive Tableau visualizations.

Business Question

Which regions, product categories, and segments drive the most value, and where are there opportunities to improve profitability?

Dataset

  • Dataset includes transactional retail data (Sample Superstore)
  • Variables include sales, profit, category, sub-category, and region
  • Customer segments and geographic data support segmentation analysis

Methodology

  • Analyzed sales and profit performance across regions
  • Compared categories and sub-categories to identify profit drivers
  • Used Tableau filters to explore trends dynamically
  • Focused on identifying high-performing and underperforming segments

Key Insight

Profitability is not evenly distributed across product categories. Some sub-categories generate strong sales but low margins, while others contribute disproportionately to profit, highlighting opportunities for strategic focus and optimization.
