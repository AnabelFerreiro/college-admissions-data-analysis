College Admissions & Performance Analysis

Project Overview

Exploratory analysis of academic factors influencing college admissions and first-year performance.

Interactive Dashboard

Key Findings

  • Admission rate is approximately 51% admitted and 49% non-admitted.
  • Admitted students have higher average SAT Math scores.
  • SAT explains 20.9% of first-year GPA variance (R² = 0.209).
  • High School GPA explains 28.3% of first-year GPA variance (R² = 0.283).
  • High School GPA is a stronger predictor of first-year performance than SAT.
